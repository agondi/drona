Our Vision

Bringing a network of teachers and students together to create a self-sustaining shared education platform.



Why we started this?

Good question! edTech in India has been HUGE. But skewed. Most focus on after school solutions as a lucrative way to circumvent the root cause of an inadequate academic experience in schools. We believe this is a band aid solution. Being students in the Indian education system ourselves, we’ve gone through the same schooling system and have had significant exposure to other education systems. We wanted to solve the big problem - the root cause behind the substandard education being meted out to every Indian in school. Our criteria for progress has only one metric - How do we make the learning in our schools better. We want to bring the learning back into the classroom. For each student. For each teacher. Every Day. All eight hours. So that no student feels the need for tuition - online or offline - again.
